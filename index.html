ABOUT US

    The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering was established in the year 1990. The department stands green with the day-to-day developments in its infrastructure as well as state-of-the-art laboratory facilities. The department is strongly supported by a crew of well experienced and highly qualified faculty members. As a part of promoting technical and research activities, the department periodically organizes various Webinars, Guest Lectures, Workshops, Symposia, and Conferences. The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering has evolved comprehensive learning approaches other than lecture classes in an integrated manner through Seminars, Industrial Visits, Paper Presentations, Inplant training, Internships, Foreign tour, and Intensive coaching. The department provides various co-curricular and extra-curricular activities among students to bring out their talents and the students have also got placed in various companies which includes TCS, Wipro, Infosys, Zoho, Revature, Facilio, Mindtree, ExcelaCom, AMI, Simulus, Capgemini, AdminDroid, Neeyamo, SmartDV, Mphasis, BYJU'S.


Vision


    To create a center of excellence in Electronics and Communication Engineering for imparting technical expertise of the highest order and to provide career focused programmes to produce globally competent professionals.


Mission


  • To provide high quality, accessible and industry interactive academic programmes.

  • To promote professional leadership through teaching and learning excellence.

  • To excel in new technologies through enhancement of research activities.
